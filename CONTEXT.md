# Nodepad Thinking Context

Nodepad is a personal thinking tool for developing a topic spatially and associatively before writing. V0 supports thinking and export; long-form composition belongs to a later context.

## Language

**Thinking Workspace**:
A self-contained map of one topic the thinker is exploring.
_Avoid_: Project, space, canvas

**Note**:
An atomic thought captured inside a Thinking Workspace, including its text and any classification, annotation, or sources attached to it.
_Avoid_: Block, tile, node

**Label**:
A thinker-assigned or suggested name used to organize Notes by subject or meaning; the thinker may always add, change, or remove it.
_Avoid_: Category

**Note Type**:
A fixed structural classification describing the role a Note plays in thought, such as claim, question, idea, reference, or thesis.
_Avoid_: Content type, category

**Annotation**:
Optional commentary attached to a Note that adds context without changing the thought itself; it may be written by the thinker or suggested by AI.
_Avoid_: Description, AI answer

**Thinking Graph**:
The set of meaningful relationships among Notes in a Thinking Workspace, independent of how those relationships are displayed.
_Avoid_: Canvas, graph view

**Relationship**:
A meaningful association between two Notes in the same Thinking Workspace, created by the thinker or suggested by AI.
_Avoid_: Edge, influenced-by ID, connection indicator

**Synthesis**:
A provisional insight that connects tensions or patterns across multiple Notes and may become a Note when accepted by the thinker.
_Avoid_: Ghost note, generated answer

**AI Assistance**:
Optional help organizing a Thinking Workspace through suggested labels, annotations, relationships, and Syntheses. Every assisted result remains editable, and the Thinking Workspace remains fully usable without it.
_Avoid_: AI mode, autopilot

**Assistance Policy**:
The per-Workspace choice that determines whether organization is manual, uses local AI, or may send a bounded set of relevant Notes to cloud AI after explicit consent.
_Avoid_: Global AI setting, silent cloud processing
